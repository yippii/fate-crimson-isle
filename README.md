# Fate: Crimson Isle

A terminal-based **roguelike dungeon crawler** set in a dark medieval world.  
Explore dangerous rooms, battle enemies, solve puzzles, and manage your stamina wisely — because **if you die, your progress is gone**.

---

## ⚔️ Features

- 🐢 **Turtle Graphics Map**
  - Visual map where the player is represented by a moving turtle
  - Tracks room progression

- 💻 **Terminal-Based Gameplay**
  - Choose actions through text input
  - Simple, fast, and immersive

- ⚡ **Stamina System**
  - Each room costs stamina to enter
  - Choose wisely between risk and reward

- ⚔️ **Turn-Based Combat**
  - Inspired by classic RPG systems
  - Choose between:
    - Attack
    - Defend
    - Use Items
    - Special abilities

- 🎒 **Loot & Inventory**
  - Collect:
    - Swords
    - Bows & Arrows
    - Gold
    - Items
  - Manage your inventory to survive longer runs

- 🧟 **Goblin Merchant**
  - Trade for items…
  - Or attack him for loot (high risk!)

- 🧩 **Puzzle Rooms**
  - Solve riddles and decipher mysterious languages
  - Unlock secrets and hidden rewards

- 🔑 **Key System**
  - Find colored keys to unlock special rooms or progress

- 💀 **Permadeath**
  - One life per run
  - Lose everything upon death

---

## 🧭 Gameplay Loop

1. Choose a room to enter (costs stamina)
2. Encounter:
   - Enemies ⚔️
   - Loot 🎒
   - Puzzles 🧩
3. Gain rewards or take damage
4. Clear enough rooms to unlock the next floor
5. Survive as long as possible
